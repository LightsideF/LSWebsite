Why Lightside

We meet the person before we address the problem

Most people come to us not quite knowing where to start. They know the situation is serious, but they don't know what the options are, what the consequences might be, or who they can trust to tell them the truth.

We are FCA-authorised debt advisers. We are not a debt management company chasing a monthly fee, and we are not a firm that pushes one solution regardless of the situation. We look at everything — income, outgoings, every creditor, every asset — and we give you an honest assessment of every option available to you.
