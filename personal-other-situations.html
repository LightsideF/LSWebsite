What happens when you contact us

You do not need to have all the answers first. Most people come to us not knowing where to start — that is exactly what the first call is for.

1

A confidential conversation

We listen. No judgement, no pressure. You tell us what’s happening and we ask what we need to understand the full picture.

2

We map the picture

We look at the full situation — not just the debt figures, but who is involved, what stage things have reached, and what is most at risk. We will tell you plainly what we think.

3

You choose

We explain every option available to you, including the ones you can pursue yourself. We tell you what we think the chances of a good outcome are. The decision is always yours.

4

We handle it

If you instruct us, we take over. We deal with creditors, courts, enforcement agents, official bodies, and lenders directly — so you can get on with your life.
